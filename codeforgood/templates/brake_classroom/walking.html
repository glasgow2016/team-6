How to cross a road?

  1. First find the safest place to cross.
  2. Stop at the curb or edge of the street.
  3. Look left, right, left and behind you and in front of you for traffic and listen.
  4. Wait until no traffic is coming and begin crossing.
  5. Keep looking for traffic until you have finished crossing.
  6. Walk, don’t run across the street.
